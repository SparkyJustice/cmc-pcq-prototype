Remove a County Court Judgment

If you don't think the CCJ should have been issued in the first place, you can apply to have it removed. This is known as having it 'set aside'.

You'll have to go to a hearing. The court will usually only remove a CCJ if it accepts you didn't know about the claim against you.

You must pay a £255 fee to make the request. You can't get the fee refunded if the court rejects your request.
