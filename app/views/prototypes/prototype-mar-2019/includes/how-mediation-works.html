After the phone call

If mediation is successful you'll make a verbal agreement over the phone. We'll email the agreement to you. You won't have to sign anything but the agreement is binding.

If either party breaks the terms, the other party can go back to the court to ask for a judgment or hearing.

If mediation fails and a hearing is needed, any mediation discussions can't be mentioned in court.
